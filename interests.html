Video Games

Games are an easy way to zone out and have fun. There is so many options out there but my favorite types of games are hardcore survival FPS games like Rust. Video games are much more enjoyable for me when playing with other friends, it's a fun way to hangout online and work together.

Single player games can also be very enjoyable, especially if they have a good and exciting story like God of War.

Fish / Aquatic Animals

I have a deep interest in anything that lives in water, I find watching fish swimming peacefully is very relaxing. I dug my own pond by hand in my back yard and stocked it with koi fish, the same ones in the picture on the right. They have grown to be twice the size they were in that photo and will most likely continue to grow even bigger.

I think anyone who likes fish would enjoy having a koi fish whether it be in a pond or a tank. They are quite resilient and smart, its not to hard to teach them tricks either. Koi only grow as big as their envirement will let them, but that also means if you put them in a big space they will grow very big.

Computers
